Executive Summary

Forever Couch Gang is a revolutionary Web3 social aggregation platform that combines blockchain technology, multi-platform content integration, and age-verified community features. We're building the future of social media where users control their data, earn rewards for engagement, and access all their content in one unified space.

As a solo developer, we've made massive strides in this Alpha phase, integrating major platforms, implementing robust security, and laying the foundation for our custom blockchain. This update showcases our progress and vision for a more connected, rewarding, and safe social experience.

Security Features

Our platform prioritizes user safety with:

  • JWT Authentication with refresh tokens
  • Rate Limiting (100 requests/minute per user)
  • CORS Protection with whitelist
  • Helmet Security Headers (CSP, HSTS, XSS protection)
  • Encrypted Token Storage and HttpOnly cookies
  • OAuth State Validation with PKCE

Platform Integrations

  • X/Twitter: Tweets, follows, profiles

  • Google/YouTube: Birthdate verification, channels, subscriptions (50), playlists
  • Instagram: Profile, media (50), engagement metrics
  • Facebook: Profile, photos (50), videos (50), friends (100)
